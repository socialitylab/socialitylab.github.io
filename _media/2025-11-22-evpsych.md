Anne was a guest on [Evolutionary Psychology (The Podcast)](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/inter-group-relations-with-anne-pisor/id1815500466?i=1000733870875), where she talked with Dave and David about what makes a group, what predicts intergroup animosity, and how to change intergroup dynamics - with examples from Bolivia and East Africa along the way.
